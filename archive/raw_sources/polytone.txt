<h2 style="text-align: center;"><strong>📖 About&nbsp;📖</strong></h2>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Polytone is a client-side <strong>customization</strong> mod which allows extensive tweaking of various aspect of the game via <strong>Resource Packs<br><br><br></strong>Mod has extensive documentation on the <a href="https://github.com/MehVahdJukaar/polytone/wiki" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener"><strong>WIKI</strong></a>, including an example pack.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">If you have questions feeel free to contact me and remmeber to add Polytone to the optional dependency section of your resource pack.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Main features are:</span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;">Custom <strong>Client Side Particles</strong> <strong>✨</strong></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;">Custom <strong>Post Shaders 🌅</strong></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong><strong>Entity Models Particle Emitters 🦋</strong></strong></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;">Custom <strong><strong>Client Side Sounds 🎵</strong></strong></span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Customize Creative Tabs 🏷️</strong></span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Config screen</strong> to disable<strong> Pack overlays 🔧</strong></span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Move GUI slots&nbsp;🗓️</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Add GUI elements</strong> 📒</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Custom Item Model</strong>🗡️</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Modify <strong>existing particles🖌️</strong></span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Add/Remove&nbsp;<strong>Item Tooltips</strong>&nbsp;🪡</strong></span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Change Item Rarity💍</strong></span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Customize&nbsp;<strong>Map Colors </strong><strong>🗺️</strong></span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Change <strong>Block Sounds 🪅</strong></span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Custom Blocks&nbsp;<strong>Colormaps&nbsp;🎨</strong></span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Customize <strong>Particle Colors🍭</strong></strong><strong><strong>&nbsp;</strong></strong></span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Block Offset Type&nbsp;📐</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Day Time-Variant Animated Textures☀️</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Fluids Colormaps&nbsp;🍸</strong></span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Custom <strong>Biome Colors &amp; particles 🌄</strong></span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Change <strong>Spawn Eggs Colors&nbsp;🐣</strong></strong></span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Custom <strong>Sky Colors&nbsp;🌈</strong></span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Register Sound Events&nbsp;🔔</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;">Change Cloud height and Sky colors&nbsp;☁️</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>And Much More!</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">It also offers backwards compatibility with the <strong>color.properties</strong> system previously offered by Optifine, allowing said features from existing packs to work aswell</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">The mod is designed to be fast and as light weight as possible while keeping the most customizaiton it can.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">Not only does it offer a replacement for color.properties system but also offer way more features and customization options that were previously not available.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">Feel free to use this banner in your Resource Packs and be sure to<strong> show us your creations</strong> here in the comments!</span><br><a href="https://legacy.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/polytone" rel="nofollow"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/788/551/polytone_banner2.png" alt="polytone_banner" width="250"></a></p>
<p>If you are using Sodium 1.20 note that some features might not work. Most noticeably one due to <a href="https://github.com/CaffeineMC/sodium-fabric/issues/2298" rel="nofollow">this Sodium issue</a>.</p>
<p>Consider using Embeddium instead.</p>
<p>Also check out this website <a href="https://github.com/LambdAurora/optifine_alternatives" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">https://github.com/LambdAurora/optifine_alternatives</a><br><br>Actively supported Polytone versions:</p>
<p><strong>1.20.1, 1.21.1, 1.21.5, 1.21.11</strong></p>
<p><strong>Please refrain from using any other version if you dont want bugs and want all the latest features.</strong></p>
