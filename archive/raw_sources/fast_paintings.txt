<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><strong>📖 About&nbsp;📖</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Not just a performance mod.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Features:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">No painting rendering lag</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">No paintings tick lag</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Customizable painting models</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Particles</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Ambient occlusion</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">More reactive when support is broken</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Paintings wont disappear when afar</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Better looking paintings as they will use mipmap</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">no more accidentally killing of paintings with the kill command</span></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: left;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">In other words Fast Painting is a <strong>performance</strong>&nbsp;mod that optimizes paintings to render way faster</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><br>To do so the mod simply replaces them with their block equivalent form.<br></span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Block paintings will use baked models instead of entity renderer which makes them <strong>as fast as they can be</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Moreover these are just blocks now so the lag associated with the painting entity ticking is gone too!</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">This approach also makes painting update immediately when you bereak their supporting block and also allows them to have particles</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Moveoveras this doubles as a <strong>cosmetic</strong> mod since it allows you to entirely customize painting models by overriding their json model with resource pack. You can do complex things like adding wooden frames around them. Fancy!<br>To do so simply edit the model to contain a json or reference model in each of its parts (i.e. "top_left" is for model to apply to the top left corner)</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><br>Oh and dont worry you can still pass right through them. Screet doors yay!</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">&nbsp;</p>
