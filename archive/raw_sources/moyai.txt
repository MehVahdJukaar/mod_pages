<li><span style="font-size: 18px;">Can be stacked&nbsp;<br><br></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;">Have a unique boom noteblock sound<br><br></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;">Can be used to make an iron golem<br><br></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;">Can eat soap<br><br></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;">Affected by gravity will deal alot of damage when falling on entities</span></li>
</ul>
