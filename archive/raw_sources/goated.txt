<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><strong>📖 About&nbsp;📖</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">Formerly "Oh My Goat!"</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">A vanilla like mod which aims to flesh out 1.18 goats a bit more.</span><br><span style="font-size: 18px;">Its main features are Ram Block, Geep, Barbaric Helmet and Chevon meat:</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><br><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/608/589/omg-slides-3.png" width="597" height="337"></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14px;"><strong><img src="https://i.imgur.com/31uKfqT.png" width="1519" height="37"></strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 24px;"><strong>🐐 Features🐐</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Ram Block</strong></span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">The Ram Block is a block-breaking oriented block crafted from goat horns. With the help of a piston, the Ram block can damage and break blocks, much like a goat ramming into a tree.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">Break speed depends on the hardness of the block. Additionally it will be able to break anything up to obsidian hardness but only iron tier blocks will drop</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">Does not work with enchantments as it's a block</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">Also inflicts knowkback and damage on entities when pushed</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Barbaric Helmet</strong></span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">The Barbaric Helmet is a life-saving piece of equipment crafted from goat horns and copper. It provides the player with strength and speed on lower health.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Chevon</strong></span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">Chevon is a new meat dropped from goats.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>The Geep</strong></span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">Geeps are the result of breeding a goat with a sheep. they cannot breed themselves, but does yield wool and chevon, and can be milked.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">Also they are more frail with less health and cannot be dyed</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Thatch</strong></span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">Decorative building block perfect for your nordic builds!</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14px;"><strong><img src="https://i.imgur.com/31uKfqT.png" width="1519" height="37"></strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 24px;"><strong>⚙️ Mod Integration⚙️</strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;">If Windswept is installed, Chevon meat will be disabled in favor of its ones</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;">Chevon can be used in some Farmers Delight recipes</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><em>Make sure you have enough ram!</em></span></p>
