<h2 style="text-align: center;">📖 About 📖</h2>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Vista is a mod that adds Televisions, Cassettes and Viewfinders allowing you to view collect gifs and place cameras in your base.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Features:</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2><strong>Televisions:</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Need to be powered on by redstone</li>
<li>Can be linked to a view finder through a hollow cassette to view what it sees</li>
<li>Can accept and display cassettes or hollow cassettes</li>
<li>Can be connected in any square size</li>
<li>Can be interacted with droppers and hoppers to swap cassettes</li>
<li>Shift click on a tv to pause it</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Cassette:</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Can be dropped by creepers killed by a pillager arrow</li>
<li>Will also rarely be found in chests</li>
<li>Each plays a unique gif. Collect them all!</li>
<li>Insert them in a TV to play them</li>
<li>New ones can be added with datapack &amp; resource packs.</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Hollow Cassette:</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Crafted with a Cassette and Echo Shard</li>
<li>Use it on a view finder to link it to it</li>
<li>Can be inserted in a TV to view the view finder content</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>View Finder:</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Can be interacted with to see a particular view</li>
<li>Click to lock it while using it</li>
<li>Scroll to zoom</li>
<li>Can have glass panes applied to have the linked TV get a particular tint effects</li>
<li>Can also equip mob heads when supplementaries is on to have the linked tv use the respective mob head shaders</li>
<li>Can be rotated remotely using a supplementaries turn table</li>
<li>View distance can be controlled with redstone</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Wave Gate:</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Creative only item (unless configs are used)</li>
<li>Can accept a URL, can be a video, image, gif, file on your machine</li>
<li>when connected to a TV via a hollow cassette it will display that media</li>
<li>Requires either FFMPEG (doenwloaded by the mod) or Watermedia mod.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Note:</strong>&nbsp;The mod has degraded visuals when using IRIS mod. This is something iris would have to fix. For best experience don't use Iris shaderpacks. The mod will have fancy <strong>crt shaders</strong> and tv turn on effects</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Adding your own cassettes:</strong></p>
<p>to add your own cassette tapes simply add files like these in assets/your pack id/vista/cassette_tape <a href="https://github.com/MehVahdJukaar/cameramod/tree/master/common/src/main/resources/data/vista/vista/cassette_tape" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">https://github.com/MehVahdJukaar/cameramod/tree/master/common/src/main/resources/data/vista/vista/cassette_tape</a> via a datapack. These can also support sounds via the "sound" and "sound_duration" fields.</p>
<p>Then add similar files in a resource pack (also check out the mod own resource pack. You'll need either a ping or gif with the file name you specified in the previous json</p>
