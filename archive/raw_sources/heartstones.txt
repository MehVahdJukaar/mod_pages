<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><strong>📖 About&nbsp;📖</strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;<span style="font-size: 18px;">The Heartstone is an item crafted with 3 Amethyst and 1 Heart Of The Sea. It comes with the following features</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;">Item comes in pairs, and allows the player to seek out another player to whom they've given the second stone.</span></li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;">Right-clicking the stone sends a particle trail in the direction of the player.</span></li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;">Getting closer to the target player will also make them glow pink, allowing you to easily spot them.</span></li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;">Using a map will also reveal the other player on it</span></li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;">If the other player dies, your own heartstone will break, repairing itself only when the other players recovers it.</span></li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 18px;">Additionally you'll be able to craft heart shaped firework charges with amethyst shards (forge only)</span></li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">Now you can find friends without hitting F3 once!</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 14px;"><iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/uRE5flchnYU?wmode=transparent" width="638" height="358" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></iframe></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">The Heartstone works in three stages: 0-999 blocks, 1,000-9,999 blocks, and 10,000+ blocks. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">As the distance between people increases, the particles indicating their direction will shrink and lower in pitch.</span></p>
