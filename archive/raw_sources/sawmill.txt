<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><strong>📖 About&nbsp;📖</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Universal Sawmill does one thing and does it well.<br>The mod adds a new Sawmill crafting station which allows you to craft any wooden items.<br>The feature is fully integrated within your world with unique Villager Houses and Carpenter villager providing a seamless experience.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Here's a list of all the features</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Sawmill Recipes are <strong>compatible with&nbsp;ANY mod</strong>, allowing to craft ANY wood-derivate block</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Carpenter Villagers</strong> will can work at a Sawmill</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Data driven carpenter trades</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Carpenter comes with <strong>5 unique houses</strong> which can spawn in your Villages, handmade to perfectly match the vanilla village style</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Sawmill workstation also features unique <strong>Sawing Sounds</strong><br></span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">KubeJs integration incase you need to add some extra recipes (you can use datapacks aswell)</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Data driven villager trades (using Moonlight villager trade system)</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">No extra wooden block spam. Just the Sawmill</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Compatible with Repurposed Structures</span></li>
<li style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Are you a modpack maker and <strong>dont want dynamic recipes generation</strong>? Just turn on&nbsp;<em>save_recipes</em> config to generate all the recipes to JSON so you can add them statically to your pack instead!</span></li>
</ul>
