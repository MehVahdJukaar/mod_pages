<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">MmmMmmMmmMmm adds a Target Dummy. Hit it and it displays the damage you dealt, so you can compare weapons, enchantments and potion effects without guessing.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">It can be equipped with armor to test damage reduction, and with mob heads to test damage types like Smite.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/mmmmmmmmmmmm.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🎯 Features 🎯</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Showcase:</strong></span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/sfYEYH5Haj4?wmode=transparent" width="638" height="358" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></iframe></p>

<p><strong>Placement</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Right-click on a block with a Target Dummy to place it</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">16 rotation directions based on where you're facing - like an armor stand</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Shift + left-click with an empty hand to pick it up</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Equipping</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Right-click to equip armor, tools, or shields - click the corresponding body part to unequip</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Equip an <strong>undead mob head</strong> to make it register as undead (for Smite testing)</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Other mob heads change its type for other damage categories</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Equip a <strong>player head</strong> to make mobs target the dummy instead of you</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Equip a <strong>pumpkin or jack o'lantern</strong> to activate <strong>Scarecrow mode</strong> - scares nearby animals</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Damage Display</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Damage numbers color-code by type: red for crits, yellow for lightning, etc.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Supports <strong>multiple simultaneous numbers</strong> with a fading animation</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">DPS shown on the status bar - choose between static or dynamic display in configs</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Rename the item to a number to set the dummy's health</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Enable damage numbers for <strong>any entity</strong> in the configs</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Boss Mode</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Give it a <strong>banner</strong> to enter Boss Mode - shows a boss health bar</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Automation</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Dispensers can place and remove dummies</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Remove by breaking the block under its feet</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Place a <strong>Target Block</strong> below the dummy to read damage dealt as a redstone signal</span></li>
</ul>
