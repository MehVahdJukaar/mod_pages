<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Smarter Farmers rewrites farmer villager behavior. Farmers replant what they just harvested, handle crops from any mod, equip a hoe before working, and stop trampling their own farmland.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">It takes priority over other mods that alter farmers, so it can be installed alongside them.</span></p>

<blockquote><p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Mob griefing must be enabled.</strong> Farmers cannot place or break a single block without it.</span></p></blockquote>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/smarter-farmers.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🌾 Features 🌾</strong></span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Plant, harvest and replant crops from any mod</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Replant what was just harvested, rather than the first seed in the inventory</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">With nothing to replant, pick a crop based on what is growing nearby, instead of leaving single-crop gaps in a field</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Accept and pick up seeds for those crops, while non-farmer villagers no longer hoard them</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Equip a hoe while harvesting, of a tier matching their villager level, and equip the seed they are about to plant</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">No longer trample farmland</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Plant pumpkins and melons in a checkerboard, leaving the air space an existing stem needs</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Work on modded farmland through the <code>farmer_plantable_on</code> block tag, which includes Supplementaries' planter by default</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Harvest crop-like blocks that are not crops through the <code>crop_replaceable</code> tag, covering Immersive Weathering's weeds and the large crops from Overweight Farming and Hefty Crops</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Take no damage from sweet berry bushes</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Fail their farm task far less often</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">All villagers, not just farmers, accept and breed with any modded food item</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/smarter-farmers.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🔍 Debugging 🔍</strong></span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Debug renderers can be enabled in the configs</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">They show what a farmer is targeting, what it plans to plant, and why its task failed</span></li>
</ul>
