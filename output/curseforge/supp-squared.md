<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Supplementaries² is the overflow drawer for <a href="https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/supplementaries">Supplementaries</a>. It's where the extra colour variants, the extra wood variants and the odd ideas that didn't quite earn a slot in the base mod ended up.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">If you already build with Supplementaries and keep wishing a block came in one more flavour, this is that mod.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/orange.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📦 What's Inside 📦</strong></span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Colored Sacks</strong>: sacks in all 16 dye colors, dyeable in the crafting grid</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Item Shelves in every wood type</strong>, instead of just the vanilla few</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Gold Candle Holders</strong>: the candle holder in gold, in every candle color including soul, ender, spectacle and cupric</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Daub Stairs, Slabs and Vertical Slabs</strong>, for both plain daub and wattle and daub</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Metal Frames</strong>, plus braces and cross braces</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Plaques</strong> in iron, gold and copper: engravable signs with their own edit screen</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Copper, Crimson, Gold and Brass Lanterns</strong></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Heavy Key</strong>: like a normal key, but it can't be renamed and is unique on craft, so it can't be duplicated</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Weighted Doormats</strong></span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/orange.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>❓ F.A.Q. ❓</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Q: Do I need Supplementaries?</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>A:</strong> Yes. This is an addon, it does nothing on its own.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Q: Why isn't this just part of Supplementaries?</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>A:</strong> Supplementaries is already enormous. Splitting off the variants and the odder additions keeps the base mod's block count sane for people who don't want them.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Q: Can I use this in my modpack?</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>A:</strong> Go ahead.</span></p>
