<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Fast Paintings replaces painting entities with blocks, rendered as baked models instead of through an entity renderer. This removes their rendering and tick cost, and fixes several long-standing painting quirks along the way.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Because paintings are now blocks with JSON models, resource packs can also change their shape.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/fast-paintings.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>⚡ Features ⚡</strong></span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Paintings render as cheaply as any other block, with no entity tick overhead</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">No longer disappear at a distance</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Mipmap support, so they look better far away</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">React immediately when their supporting block is broken</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Cannot be destroyed by the <code>/kill</code> command</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Still walk-through, so secret doors behind paintings keep working</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/fast-paintings.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🖼️ Customization 🖼️</strong></span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Each painting part (<code>top_left</code>, <code>top_right</code> and so on) has its own model JSON that a resource pack can replace</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Any geometry works, so frames, borders and rounded corners are all possible</span></li>
</ul>
