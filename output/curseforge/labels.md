<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Labels mark what is inside your storage. They work like Item Frames, but sit flush against the block, stick to any surface, and let you interact with the block behind them.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Place one, then shift-click it with an item to set what it shows. Icons are drawn in a flat, hand-painted style rather than as a floating 3D item.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/labels.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🏷️ Features 🏷️</strong></span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Stick to floors, ceilings, walls, slabs, stairs and fences, with no flat surface required</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Clicking a Label passes the interaction through to the block behind it, so labelled chests and barrels still open</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Apply a <strong>Glow Ink Sac</strong> to make one visible in the dark</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Dye them in any of the 16 colors</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Shift-click with a <strong>Feather</strong> to show the item's name instead of its icon</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Clicking with a stack updates the displayed item. Labels hold no inventory of their own</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Rendering style is configurable, and overridable with a resource pack</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/labels.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>❓ F.A.Q. ❓</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Q: Can I put a Label on a chest without blocking access?</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>A:</strong> Yes - clicking the Label interacts with the block behind it, so your chest opens normally.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Q: Does it work on modded blocks?</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>A:</strong> Labels can be placed on any surface that accepts a block entity interaction. Works with most modded storage blocks.</span></p>
