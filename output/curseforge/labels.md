<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Labels are like Item Frames - except they work on every surface and shape, sit flush against the block, and let you interact with whatever is behind them.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Place a Label on any surface, then shift-click it with an item to set what it displays. The displayed icon is rendered in a pixelated, colored style that looks deliberately hand-made.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/green.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🏷️ Features 🏷️</strong></span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Universal placement</strong> - sticks to floors, ceilings, walls, slabs, stairs, and fences; no flat wall required</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Transparent interaction</strong> - clicking a Label passes through to the block behind it, so you can open a chest, pull a lever, or access a barrel without removing the label</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Glow Ink Sac</strong> - right-click a placed Label with Glow Ink to make it visible in the dark</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Dyeable</strong> - right-click with any dye to tint the label's background color; 16 colors available</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Feather</strong> - shift-click with a Feather to toggle display of the held item's name instead of its icon</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Item stacks</strong> - clicking with a stack updates the display item; the Label itself retains no inventory</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Fully configurable rendering style via configs and resource packs</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/green.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>❓ F.A.Q. ❓</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Q: Can I put a Label on a chest without blocking access?</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>A:</strong> Yes - clicking the Label interacts with the block behind it, so your chest opens normally.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Q: Does it work on modded blocks?</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>A:</strong> Labels can be placed on any surface that accepts a block entity interaction. Works with most modded storage blocks.</span></p>
