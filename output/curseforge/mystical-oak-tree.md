<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🌳 About 🌳</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Mystical Oak Tree adds a rare talking tree to the Overworld. It looks almost like a normal oak, and clicking it starts a conversation.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">It tracks how you treat it. Talk to it regularly and it opens up; wake it during the day or attack it and it remembers.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Made for Spooky Jam 2022.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/mystical-oak-tree.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🌳 Features 🌳</strong></span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Generates rarely in any Overworld biome, recognisable by a slightly different tree shape</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Right-click to talk to it, once per day</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Waking it during the day costs trust, and it holds a grudge</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Attacking it is a bad idea</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Over 300 hand-written lines, plus content fetched dynamically, so it rarely repeats itself</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Talks about: random facts, tree jokes, Minecraft lore and trivia, words of wisdom, video game quotes, tips, memes, tomorrow's weather forecast, and you</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/mystical-oak-tree.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>
