<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">The Heartstone is a craftable item that lets you track and stay connected to another player - no F3 required.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Crafted from 3 Amethyst Shards and 1 Heart of the Sea, it comes in pairs. Give one to a friend and keep the other.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/uRE5flchnYU?wmode=transparent" width="638" height="358" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></iframe></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/heartstone.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>💜 Features 💜</strong></span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Right-click</strong> the stone to send a particle trail in the direction of your paired player</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Getting <strong>closer</strong> to your partner makes them glow pink, so you can spot them even through walls</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Using a <strong>Map</strong> reveals the other player's position on it</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">The stone works in <strong>three distance stages</strong>: 0–999 blocks, 1,000–9,999 blocks, and 10,000+ blocks - the particles shrink and shift in pitch as distance grows</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">If your partner <strong>dies</strong>, your heartstone breaks - it repairs itself only when they pick theirs back up</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Craft heart-shaped <strong>Firework Charges</strong> using Amethyst Shards <em>(Forge only)</em></span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/heartstone.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>❓ F.A.Q. ❓</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Q: Does it work cross-dimension?</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>A:</strong> Yes - it tracks your partner across dimensions, though the direction pointer only works if you're in the same dimension.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Q: What happens if I lose my heartstone?</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>A:</strong> If the <em>other</em> player dies, your stone breaks. If you lose your own, you'll need to reclaim it (it won't function while on the ground).</span></p>
