<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Snowy Spirit is a winter-themed mod originally made for Winter Jam 2021. It adds rideable Sleds, a full gingerbread building set, festive decorations, and seasonal villager behavior.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">It also adds the Gingerbread Golem, a pet that follows you around and can be eaten.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/snowy-spirit.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>❄️ Features ❄️</strong></span></p>

<p><strong>Sleds</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Ride downhill, accelerating with the slope, with special handling on ice and through curves</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Available in every vanilla wood type</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Attach a Chest or Shulker Box for storage, or a Wolf as a passenger</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Add a carpet on top to change the look and stop powder snow from freezing you</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Generate inside igloos</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Gingerbread Golems</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Built by placing a Carved Pumpkin on a Gingerbread Block and adding a Gumdrop button</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Follow you around as a pet, in 16 colors with their own personalities</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Can be eaten when you are hungry, and repaired by feeding them Gingerbread Cookies</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Vulnerable to Milk and Water, so keep them out of the rain</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Gingerbread Houses generate as structures, with a Witch and some Golems inside</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Seasonal Villagers</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Adults hang <strong>Wreaths</strong> on their doors at the start of winter and take them down after</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Baby villagers throw snowballs at each other and at players</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">With <strong>Supplementaries</strong> installed, villagers hand out Presents</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Defaults to December 20th through January 30th</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Ginger</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">A crop that grows in Sparse Jungle biomes, found as Wild Ginger</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Bakes into <strong>Gingerbread</strong>, the main winter building material</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Gingerbread comes as stairs, slabs, doors, trapdoors and a Frosted variant</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Also makes Gingerbread Cookies and Eggnog</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Storable in a Ginger Crate, and plantable in a flower pot</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Decorations</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Gumdrops</strong>: sugar blocks in all 16 dye colors that act as both a button and a pressure plate, placeable on any face</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Candy Cane</strong> blocks for festive building</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Wreaths</strong>: hang on any door, including modded ones</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Glow Lights</strong>: small lights that attach to leaves, removable with Shears, including a Prismatic variant</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Snow Globe</strong>: creates a localized snowy state in snow biomes</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">A winter-themed music disc</span></li>
</ul>
