<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Snowy Spirit is a winter-themed mod born from Winter Jam 2021. It adds Sleds for speeding downhill, festive new blocks, seasonal villager behaviors, and the Gingerbread Golem — a pet that you can also eat.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/6lPE3bSGgok?wmode=transparent" width="638" height="358" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></iframe></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/blue.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🛷 Sleds 🛷</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Sleds let you ride the terrain — they accelerate downhill, have special behavior on ice, and can navigate curves. Attach a <strong>Chest or Shulker Box</strong> for storage, or a <strong>Wolf</strong> as a passenger.</span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Igloos now spawn with a Sled inside</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Add <strong>carpet</strong> on top to customize the look and prevent powder snow from freezing you</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Multiple wood types and material variants available</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/blue.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🏘️ Seasonal Villagers 🏘️</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">During winter (December 20 – January 30 by default), villagers participate in the season:</span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Adults hang <strong>Wreaths</strong> on their doors at the start of winter and remove them after</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Baby villagers throw <strong>Snowballs</strong> at each other and at players</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">If <strong>Supplementaries</strong> is installed, villagers also give out <strong>Presents</strong></span></li>
</ul>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Serene Seasons</strong> is supported — link the seasonal behavior to in-game seasons instead of real-world dates.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/blue.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🍬 New Blocks & Items 🍬</strong></span></p>

<p><strong>🫚 Ginger</strong></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">A new crop that grows in Sparse Jungle biomes. Harvest Ginger and bake it into <strong>Gingerbread Blocks</strong> — the primary crafting material for winter builds and golems.</span></p>

<p><strong>🔵 Gumdrops</strong></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Colorful sugar blocks available in all 16 dye colors. They act as a button <em>and</em> a pressure plate — press them to send a signal. Can be placed in any direction, making them useful for redstone contraptions and decoration both.</span></p>

<p><strong>🍬 Candy Cane</strong></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Purely decorative blocks for building festive structures.</span></p>

<p><strong>🌿 Wreaths</strong></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Hang them on any door, including modded doors. Automatically placed by villagers during winter.</span></p>

<p><strong>💡 Glow Lights</strong></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Small lights that attach to leaves. Remove them with Shears.</span></p>

<p><strong>🌨️ Snow Globe</strong></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">A decorative block that creates a localized snowy state in snow biomes.</span></p>

<p><strong>🎵 Music Disc</strong></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">A new winter-themed music disc.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/blue.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🍪 Gingerbread Golems 🍪</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Place a Carved Pumpkin on top of a Gingerbread Block and add a Gumdrop button to bring it to life. Gingerbread Golems will follow you as a pet.</span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Come in <strong>16 colors</strong> with distinct personalities</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Can be <strong>eaten</strong> when you're hungry (repairs when fed Gingerbread Cookies, obviously)</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Vulnerable to <strong>Milk</strong> and <strong>Water</strong> — keep them away from rain</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Gingerbread Houses</strong> can generate as structures, with a Witch and some Golems inside</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/blue.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>
