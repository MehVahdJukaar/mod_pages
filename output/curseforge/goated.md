<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Formerly <em>"Oh My Goat!"</em> — Goated is a vanilla-style mod that fleshes out the 1.18 Goat a bit more. New blocks, a new mob, a new food, and a piece of equipment that gets better when you're losing.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><em>Make sure you have enough ram!</em></span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/green.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🐐 Features 🐐</strong></span></p>

<p><strong>🪨 Ram Block</strong></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">A block-breaking machine crafted from goat horns. Push it with a piston and it rams into blocks, breaking them just like a goat hitting a tree.</span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Break speed depends on block hardness; can break anything up to obsidian hardness, but only iron-tier blocks drop</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Does not work with enchantments — it's a block, not a tool</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Also knocks back and damages any entities it hits</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>⛑️ Barbaric Helmet</strong></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Crafted from goat horns and copper. Grants Strength and Speed when you're on low health — the worse you're doing, the better you fight.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><em>Credit to Ariboa12 for the helmet idea.</em></span></p>

<p><strong>🥩 Chevon</strong></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Goats now drop Chevon, a new meat. Integrates with Farmer's Delight recipes.</span></p>

<p><strong>🐑 The Geep</strong></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">The result of breeding a Goat with a Sheep. Can't breed themselves, but yield Wool and Chevon, and can be milked. More fragile than either parent — lower health and can't be dyed.</span></p>

<p><strong>🌾 Thatch</strong></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">A decorative building block perfect for Nordic-style builds.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/green.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>⚙️ Mod Integration ⚙️</strong></span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">If <strong>Windswept</strong> is installed, Chevon is disabled in favor of its own goat drops</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Chevon works in <strong>Farmer's Delight</strong> recipes</span></li>
</ul>
