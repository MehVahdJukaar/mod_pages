<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Formerly "Oh My Goat!", Goated is a vanilla-styled mod that builds out the Goat. It adds a block-breaking machine made from goat horns, a helmet that gets stronger as your health drops, goat meat, and a Goat-Sheep hybrid.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/goated.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🐐 Features 🐐</strong></span></p>

<p><strong>Ram Block</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Pushed by a piston, it rams into blocks and breaks them</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Break speed depends on block hardness. Breaks anything up to obsidian hardness, but only iron-tier blocks drop</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Enchantments have no effect, since it is a block and not a tool</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Knocks back and damages entities it hits</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Barbaric Helmet</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Grants Strength and Speed while you are on low health, scaling as your health drops</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Made from goat horns and copper</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Helmet idea by <strong>Ariboa12</strong></span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>The Geep</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Bred from a Goat and a Sheep</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Drops Wool and Chevon, and can be milked</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Cannot breed with each other, cannot be dyed, and has less health than either parent</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Chevon</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">A new meat dropped by goats</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Works in Farmer's Delight recipes</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Thatch</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">A decorative building block, suited to Nordic-style builds</span></li>
</ul>
