<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Universal Sawmill adds a <strong>Sawmill</strong> crafting station for crafting any wooden item from any installed mod. It's fully integrated into the world: Carpenter villagers work at the Sawmill, and their handcrafted houses generate naturally in villages.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Requires <strong><a href="https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/selene">Moonlight Library</a></strong>.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/blue.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🪚 Features 🪚</strong></span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Sawmill recipes are <strong>compatible with any mod</strong> — craft any wood-derivative block automatically</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Carpenter Villager</strong> works at the Sawmill with data-driven trades</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>5 unique Carpenter houses</strong> generate in villages, handcrafted to match the vanilla style</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Unique <strong>sawing sounds</strong></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">KubeJS integration for custom recipes (datapacks also supported)</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>No extra wooden block spam</strong> — just the Sawmill block itself</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Compatible with <strong>Repurposed Structures</strong></span></li>
</ul>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Enable <code>save_recipes</code> in the config to export all dynamically generated recipes to JSON files — useful for static inclusion in your pack or for tweaking individual recipes.</span></p>
