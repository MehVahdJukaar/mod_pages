<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Supplementaries is a large, highly configurable Vanilla+ mod adding functional and decorative content designed to look like it shipped with the base game. It fills gaps Mojang left: storage that isn't a chest, lighting that isn't a torch, redstone parts that aren't a repeater, and a lot of decoration.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">It adds over 200 blocks and items, almost all of which can be individually disabled. The <a href="https://github.com/MehVahdJukaar/Supplementaries/wiki"><strong>Wiki</strong></a> documents each one.</span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Requires <strong><a href="https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/selene">Moonlight Library</a></strong></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">The Fabric version is supported, but may be missing some minor features</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Free to include in modpacks</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/supplementaries.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>✨ Features ✨</strong></span></p>

<p><strong>Storage</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Sack</strong>: a cheap portable container that slows you down when carried</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Safe</strong>: locked with a <strong>Key</strong>, so only its owner can open it. Also a <strong>Lock Block</strong> for doors</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Jar</strong> and <strong>Tinted Jar</strong>: store fluids, cookies, or small mobs. Also a Boat in a Jar and a Tater in a Jar</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Item Shelf</strong>, <strong>Book Pile</strong>, <strong>Crystal Display</strong> and <strong>Hat Stand</strong> for showing things off</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Quiver</strong> for arrows, <strong>Lunch Basket</strong> for food</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Present</strong> and <strong>Trapped Present</strong> in all 16 colors</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Urn</strong> for ashes</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Redstone</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Bellows</strong>: pushes air to feed fire and speed up furnaces</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Crank</strong> and <strong>Turn Table</strong>: rotate blocks and machines</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Faucet</strong>: moves fluids between containers, no pipes needed</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Pulley Block</strong>: raise and lower ropes and whatever hangs from them</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Relayer</strong>, <strong>Speaker Block</strong>, <strong>Redstone Illuminator</strong>, <strong>Sliding Block</strong>, <strong>Cog Block</strong>, <strong>Clock Block</strong></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Spring Launcher</strong> for launching entities, <strong>Sconce Lever</strong> for a lever that lights the room</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Pedestal</strong> and <strong>Wind Vane</strong></span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Lighting</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Sconces</strong> in Glow, Soul, Ender, Cupric and Nether Brass variants</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Candle Holders</strong> in all 16 colors, plus Soul, Ender, Spectacle and Cupric</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Gold Candelabra</strong>, <strong>Skull Candle</strong>, <strong>Fire Pit</strong></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Lamps</strong> in Stone, Blackstone, Deepslate and End Stone</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Lumisene</strong>: a glowing fluid, bottled or in a bucket</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Decoration</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Rope</strong> and <strong>Roped Post</strong>: climbable, and hang things from them</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Flag</strong>, <strong>Bunting</strong>, <strong>Awning</strong>, <strong>Doormat</strong> in every dye color</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Globe</strong>: shows a map of your world, with a Sepia variant</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Blackboard</strong> for drawing, <strong>Notice Board</strong> for posting written books</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Way Sign</strong> for pointing at nearby structures, <strong>Statue</strong> for decoration</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Flower Box</strong> and <strong>Planter</strong> for growing things indoors</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Timber Frames</strong>, <strong>Daub</strong> and <strong>Wattle and Daub</strong> for medieval building</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Gold</strong> and <strong>Netherite</strong> doors, trapdoors, gates and bars</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Stone Tiles, Blackstone Tiles, Ash Bricks, Lapis Bricks, Gravel Bricks, Brittlestone, Fine Wood, Checker Block, Raked Gravel</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Tools and Gadgets</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Slingshot</strong>: fires blocks, and can be enchanted for special effects</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Bomb</strong>, <strong>Blue Bomb</strong> and <strong>Spiky Bomb</strong> for demolition</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Cannon</strong> and <strong>Cannonball</strong>, mountable on boats and rafts</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Rope Arrow</strong>: shoot a rope up a wall and climb it</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Altimeter</strong>, <strong>Speedometer</strong>, <strong>Hourglass</strong>, <strong>Wrench</strong></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Flute</strong> to call your pets, <strong>Confetti Popper</strong> for celebrating</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Bubble Blower</strong> for making bubble blocks</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Soap</strong>: strips color off dyed and waxed blocks</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Antique Ink</strong>, <strong>Cartographer's Quill</strong> and <strong>Slice Map</strong> for writing and cartography</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Goblet</strong>, <strong>Pancake</strong>, <strong>Candy</strong>, <strong>Sugar Cube</strong></span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Mobs and World</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Plunderer</strong>: a hostile illager variant</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Red Merchant</strong>: a rare trading mob</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Flax</strong>: a new crop, growing wild, for string and cloth</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Bamboo Spikes</strong>, including a Tipped variant, and <strong>Cage</strong> for trapping mobs</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>❓ F.A.Q. ❓</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Q: How do I disable or tweak a feature?</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>A:</strong> Refer to the Customization section in the <a href="https://github.com/MehVahdJukaar/Supplementaries/wiki"><strong>Wiki</strong></a> or join our <a href="https://discord.com/invite/qdKRTDf8Cv"><strong>Discord</strong></a>.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Q: Could you update/downgrade to version X?</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>A:</strong> I plan to port the mod to every new major Minecraft version eventually, be patient. Downgrades are not happening. Maintaining a single version already takes an enormous amount of time, and so much changed between versions that it would mean re-learning modding from scratch.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Q: Could you add feature/block X?</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>A:</strong> Join our <a href="https://discord.com/invite/qdKRTDf8Cv">Discord</a>, we have a dedicated suggestions section. If the idea fits the mod well it has a good chance of eventually being added.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Q: How do I use block/item X?</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>A:</strong> Refer to the <a href="https://github.com/MehVahdJukaar/Supplementaries/wiki"><strong>Wiki</strong></a>.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Q: The Fabric version is missing feature X</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>A:</strong> Supplementaries is primarily a Forge mod. Missing Fabric features didn't have a straightforward implementation because Fabric lacked a counterpart to something the Forge API provides. For the full picture, see <a href="https://i.imgur.com/JdipbyU.jpg">this</a>.</span></p>
