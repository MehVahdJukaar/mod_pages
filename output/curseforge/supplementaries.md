<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Supplementaries is a large, highly configurable Vanilla+ mod focused on adding functional and decorative content that feels at home in the base game. Every feature is designed to be aesthetically viable and complementary to existing content, filling gaps that Mojang left behind.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">The mod adds over 80 blocks and items across many categories: rope, jars, sign posts, pedestals, sconces, notice boards, wind chimes, globes, plushies, bamboo spikes, turnstiles, and much more. Check the <a href="https://github.com/MehVahdJukaar/Supplementaries/wiki"><strong>Wiki</strong></a> for the full feature list.</span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Requires <strong><a href="https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/selene">Moonlight Library</a></strong></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">While supported, the Fabric version may be missing some minor features</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Free to include in modpacks</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>❓ F.A.Q. ❓</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Q: How do I disable or tweak a feature?</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>A:</strong> Refer to the Customization section in the <a href="https://github.com/MehVahdJukaar/Supplementaries/wiki"><strong>Wiki</strong></a> or join our <a href="https://discord.com/invite/qdKRTDf8Cv"><strong>Discord</strong></a>.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Q: Could you update/downgrade to version X?</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>A:</strong> I plan to port the mod to every new major Minecraft version eventually, be patient. Downgrades are not happening. Maintaining a single version already takes an enormous amount of time, and so much changed between versions that it would mean re-learning modding from scratch.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Q: Could you add feature/block X?</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>A:</strong> Join our <a href="https://discord.com/invite/qdKRTDf8Cv">Discord</a>, we have a dedicated suggestions section. If the idea fits the mod well it has a good chance of eventually being added.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Q: How do I use block/item X?</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>A:</strong> Refer to the <a href="https://github.com/MehVahdJukaar/Supplementaries/wiki"><strong>Wiki</strong></a>.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Q: The Fabric version is missing feature X</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>A:</strong> Supplementaries is primarily a Forge mod. Missing Fabric features didn't have a straightforward implementation because Fabric lacked a counterpart to something the Forge API provides. For the full picture, see <a href="https://i.imgur.com/JdipbyU.jpg">this</a>.</span></p>
