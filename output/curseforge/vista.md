<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Vista adds working screens to Minecraft. Televisions play Cassettes, and can be linked to a Viewfinder placed anywhere in the world to show a live camera feed of it. TVs can be tiled into screens of any size.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Screens render through a CRT filter with scanlines, vignette and a turn-on animation. The mod also adds reflective Mirrors, Picture Tapes for displaying maps and photos, and a Wave Gate that streams media from a URL.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/vista.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📺 Features 📺</strong></span></p>

<p><strong>Television</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Needs a redstone signal to turn on</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Adjacent TVs merge into one big screen, up to 8x8 by default, 24x24 max</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Accepts cassettes, picture tapes, or a hollow cassette for a live feed</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Works with hoppers and droppers</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Shift-click to pause</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Optionally consumes Forge energy on NeoForge</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Cassettes</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Each plays a different looping animation. About 20 are included</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Dropped by creepers killed by a pillager</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">3% chance in rare loot chests: woodland mansions, stronghold libraries, jungle temples, igloos, bastions, shipwrecks, nether fortresses, trail ruins, ominous trial chamber rewards</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Hollow Cassette</strong>: bind it to a Viewfinder, then play it in a TV for the live feed</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Viewfinder</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Right-click opens its screen: lens slot, pitch and yaw controls, view button</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Sneak-click to look through it directly</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Scroll to zoom, click to lock the view</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">A glass pane in the lens slot tints the linked TV's output</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">View distance controllable with redstone</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Can be aimed by a Supplementaries turntable or a ComputerCraft computer</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Keeps broadcasting from Create contraptions (NeoForge only) and Sable sublevels</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Mirror</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Render a real reflection of the world, not a static texture</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Tile into larger surfaces, like TVs</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Placed flush with the block face or recessed into it, depending on where you click</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Reflect other mirrors recursively, with correct parallax. Depth and quality falloff are configurable</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Work on Sable sublevels</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Made with <strong>Crystalline</strong>, dropped by elder guardians</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Picture Tape</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Holds up to 32 pictures and plays them as a slideshow on any TV</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Right-click to load pictures and set playback speed</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Accepts filled maps and vanilla paintings, plus Exposure photographs and albums and Joy of Painting canvases</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Wave Gate</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Set it to any URL or local file (video, image or GIF) to play it on a linked TV</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Creative-only by default, craftable via config</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Needs FFmpeg, which the mod offers to download on first launch, or the WaterMedia mod</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">URL allowlist config for servers</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Exposed as a ComputerCraft peripheral</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Misc</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Looking at an enderman through a TV angers it. Killing an enderman angered this way drops the <strong>Sojourn</strong> music disc</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Playing a cassette on a large screen grants the <strong>Absolute Cinema</strong> advancement</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🛠️ For Pack Makers 🛠️</strong></span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Cassette tapes are a datapack registry, no code needed</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Put JSON definitions in <code>data/<your_pack>/vista/cassette_tape/</code> and the matching GIF or PNG in a resource pack</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Definitions support <code>sound</code> and <code>sound_duration</code></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">See the <a href="https://github.com/MehVahdJukaar/cameramod/tree/master/common/src/main/resources/data/vista/vista/cassette_tape">mod's own tapes</a> for reference</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Mirrors, picture tapes and the wave gate can each be disabled entirely in the config, along with screen size limits and the rendering pipeline</span></li>
</ul>
