<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Vista adds <strong>Televisions, Cassettes, and Viewfinders</strong> to Minecraft — letting you collect GIF cassette tapes, set up live camera feeds in your base, and watch them play back on big screens powered by redstone.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">TVs render with authentic <strong>CRT shaders</strong> and turn-on effects. Screens can be arranged in any square grid size. Cassettes are rare loot, each playing a unique GIF — collect them all.</span></p>

<blockquote><p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Note:</strong> The mod has degraded visuals when using Iris shader packs. For the best experience (CRT shaders, turn-on effects), play without Iris.</span></p></blockquote>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/magenta.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📺 Televisions 📺</strong></span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Must be powered by redstone to function</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Accept cassette tapes or hollow cassettes</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Can be arranged in any square grid size for a bigger screen</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Compatible with droppers and hoppers for automated cassette swapping</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Shift-click a TV to pause playback</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Link to a Viewfinder via a hollow cassette to display a live camera feed</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/magenta.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📼 Cassettes 📼</strong></span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Dropped by creepers killed by a pillager arrow</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Also found rarely in loot chests</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Each cassette plays a unique GIF</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Insert in a TV to play</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">New cassettes can be added via datapacks and resource packs</span></li>
</ul>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Hollow Cassette:</strong></span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Crafted from a Cassette + Echo Shard</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Use on a Viewfinder to link the two together</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Insert in a TV to display the live Viewfinder feed</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/magenta.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📷 Viewfinder 📷</strong></span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Right-click to look through the viewfinder's perspective</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Click to lock the view; scroll to zoom</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Apply glass panes to tint the linked TV's output</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Equip mob heads (with Supplementaries installed) to apply mob-head shaders to the TV</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Remotely rotate using a Supplementaries turntable</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">View distance controllable with redstone</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/magenta.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🌊 Wave Gate 🌊</strong></span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Creative-only item (configurable)</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Accepts any URL: video, image, GIF, or a local file</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Connect to a TV via a hollow cassette to display any media</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Requires <strong>FFMPEG</strong> (auto-downloaded) or the <strong>Watermedia</strong> mod</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/magenta.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🛠️ Custom Cassettes 🛠️</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><em>For datapack and resource pack makers.</em></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Add JSON definition files to <code>data/your_pack_id/vista/cassette_tape/</code> in a datapack. See the <a href="https://github.com/MehVahdJukaar/cameramod/tree/master/common/src/main/resources/data/vista/vista/cassette_tape">mod's examples on GitHub</a>. Definitions support <code>sound</code> and <code>sound_duration</code> fields.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Then add the corresponding GIF or PNG to a resource pack matching the filename from the JSON. Check the mod's built-in resource pack for the full structure.</span></p>
