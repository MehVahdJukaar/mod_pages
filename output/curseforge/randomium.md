<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Randomium is an ore that drops a completely random item when broken, anything from bread to a Dragon Egg. It needs at least an iron pickaxe to mine, and it does not want to be mined.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/randomium.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🎲 Features 🎲</strong></span></p>

<p><strong>Randomium Ore</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Drops one random item from the whole game when broken</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Requires an iron pickaxe or better</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Teleports or slides away when a player tries to mine it</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Randomium Crystals</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">A rarer variant that lets you choose the drop</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Combine one with any item in a crafting grid, and the resulting Crystal drops that item</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Anti-Duplication</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Item inventories are blacklisted, so the ore cannot drop a filled Shulker Box</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Anything with <code>randomium:randomium</code> in its NBT is excluded from the drop pool automatically</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/randomium.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>⚙️ Configuration ⚙️</strong></span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Drop pool, evasion behavior and ore generation are all configurable</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">The <code>randomium:blacklist</code> item tag excludes specific items from ever dropping</span></li>
</ul>
