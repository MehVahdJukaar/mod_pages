<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Randomium is a new ore that drops a completely random item when broken. Any item in the game - from bread to a Dragon Egg. Requires at least an iron pickaxe to mine.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/indigo.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🎲 Features 🎲</strong></span></p>

<p><strong>Randomium Crystals</strong></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">A rarer find, Randomium Crystals let you influence what you get. Combine one in a crafting table with any item of your choice - the resulting Crystal will drop that item when broken.</span></p>

<p><strong>✨ Shy Block</strong></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Randomium blocks have a self-defense mechanism: they may teleport or slide away when a player tries to mine them. Good luck.</span></p>

<p><strong>Anti-Duplication</strong></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">The mod blacklists item inventories (so you can't get Shulker Boxes full of items from the ore), and anything containing <code>randomium:randomium</code> in its NBT is automatically excluded from the drop pool. Configurable via the <code>randomium:blacklist</code> tag.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/indigo.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>⚙️ Configuration ⚙️</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Most behaviors - drop pool, shy mechanics, ore generation - are configurable. Use the <code>randomium:blacklist</code> item tag to exclude specific items from ever dropping.</span></p>
