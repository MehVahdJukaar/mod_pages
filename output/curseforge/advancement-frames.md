<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Advancement Frames lets you display your hard-earned Advancements as decorative wall plaques.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">The mod adds two items: the <strong>Advancement Frame</strong> and the <strong>Stat Frame</strong>, both crafted with an Emerald and some Sticks.</span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Place a frame, open its GUI, and select any advancement you've earned — or leave it empty</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">The block displays the advancement icon and shows the advancement name and the player who earned it when looked at</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Shift-clicking the placed frame shows the advancement <strong>description</strong> instead</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">If you lose an advancement, it's automatically cleared from the frame on the next world/chunk save</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Stat Frame</strong> works the same way for player statistics</span></li>
</ul>
