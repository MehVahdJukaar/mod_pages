<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Sleep Tight overhauls sleeping to make it feel dangerous, rewarding, and actually meaningful.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Early game is slower — sleep has a cooldown and costs hunger. Stick with your home bed and it becomes your sanctuary, granting bonus XP over time. Push too far and nightmares start, bad encounters spawn near your bed, and bedbugs move in. There's also a daytime hammock for naps, a portable Night Bag for adventurers, and plenty of configs if you want to dial anything back.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Uunei1ZoW-s?wmode=transparent" width="638" height="358" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></iframe></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/orange.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🎒 Night Bags 🎒</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">A portable sleeping bag crafted from Echo Shards and Phantom Membranes. Unroll it anywhere for an immediate sleep — no spawn point set, and no cooldown. Perfect for long expeditions.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/orange.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🪑 Hammocks 🪑</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">A block for napping during the day. Attach it to fences (all variants), then lie down and swing with <strong>A/D</strong> keys. Available in all 16 dye colors. Sleeping in a hammock skips to the next morning without the normal bed effects.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/orange.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🐛 Bedbugs 🐛</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">On higher difficulty, there's a chance of Bedbugs spawning after you sleep. They infest your bed over time and can crawl through doors. Cure an infested bed by dousing it with a <strong>Potion of Harming</strong>. They're persistent, so don't ignore them.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/orange.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🏠 Home Bed 🏠</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Sleep in the same bed for 6 or more nights and it becomes your <strong>Home Bed</strong>. This unlocks the <strong>Invigorated</strong> effect, which grants bonus XP for actions and slowly levels up to 100 over time. Travel or use a different bed and it resets.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/orange.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>😱 Nightmares 😱</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Consecutive nights of sleep stack a hidden <strong>Nightmare</strong> counter. When it peaks, you wake up early with a sleep cooldown that stacks the more it happens. Prevent nightmares by using <strong>Dreamer's Essence</strong>, a craftable item that attracts phantoms away from you during the night.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/orange.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>⚔️ Sleep Encounters ⚔️</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">When you sleep, the mod checks for dark spots near your bed and can spawn a hostile mob there. Keep the area lit if you want a quiet night.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/orange.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>💤 Sleep Changes 💤</strong></span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">You can <strong>lie in bed</strong> without committing to sleep (right-click vs hold)</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Sleeping has a <strong>5-minute cooldown</strong> between uses</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">A HUD element shows the <strong>time remaining</strong> until the next sleep is allowed</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Sleeping in the Nether causes less intense explosions (a small compensation for the danger)</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Villagers close their eyes when sleeping nearby</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/orange.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>⚙️ Sleep Benefits & Requirements ⚙️</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Each sleep surface — regular Bed, Hammock, Night Bag — has its own configurable sleep profile:</span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Hunger requirement</strong> — sleeping may require a certain food level</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>XP toll</strong> — each sleep can cost experience levels</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Status effects</strong> — awarded on waking (e.g., Refreshed, Well-Rested)</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Healing</strong> — restore health on waking</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Effect clearing</strong> — optionally clears negative effects on sleep</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/orange.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🧩 Mod Integration 🧩</strong></span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Heartstone</strong> — sleeping near your bonded partner grants Regeneration on waking</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/orange.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🧰 Easy Mode 🧰</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">An alternate jar is available with softer settings: no cooldown, fewer nightmares, rarer bedbugs, easier Night Bags, and no hunger consumption. Drop it in instead of the main jar if you want the features without the difficulty.</span></p>
