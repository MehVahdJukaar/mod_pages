<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Sleep Tight overhauls sleeping. Beds gain a cooldown and a hunger cost, so skipping the night is no longer free, and sleeping in the same bed repeatedly turns it into a Home Bed that grants bonus XP.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Sleeping too often has consequences: nightmares wake you early, hostile mobs spawn in dark spots near your bed, and bedbugs infest it. The mod also adds hammocks for daytime naps and a portable Night Bag for expeditions.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Everything is configurable, and an Easy Mode jar is available if you want the content without the difficulty.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/sleep-tight.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>💤 Features 💤</strong></span></p>

<p><strong>Home Bed</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Sleeping in the same bed for 6 nights makes it your Home Bed</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Grants the <strong>Invigorated</strong> effect, increasing XP gained from mobs and blocks</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Levels up with every night slept there, up to level 100</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Sleeping elsewhere resets your progress</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Optionally, respawning can be restricted to your Home Bed only</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Nightmares</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Consecutive nights of sleep build up a hidden Dread counter</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">At its peak you wake up early with a sleep cooldown, which stacks the more it happens</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Dreamer Essence</strong> wards off nightmares and attracts phantoms away from you</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Current Dread and bed familiarity are shown in the sleep HUD</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Bedbugs</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Can spawn in your bed after sleeping, more likely on higher difficulty</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Infest the bed over time and can crawl through doors</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Cured by dousing the bed with a <strong>Potion of Harming</strong></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Half of all woodland mansion beds generate already infested</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Persistent, so they will not despawn on their own</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Sleep Encounters</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Sleeping checks for dark spots near your bed and can spawn a hostile mob there</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Keeping the area lit prevents it</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Hammocks</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Attach to fences of any wood type, in all 16 dye colors</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">For daytime naps: resting in one skips to the next morning without the usual bed effects</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Swing with the <strong>A</strong> and <strong>D</strong> keys</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Falling off one earns the <strong>Inertia is a Property of Matter</strong> advancement</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Night Bags</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">A portable bed for expeditions, unrolled anywhere for an immediate sleep</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Does not set your spawn point and has no cooldown</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">By default they can only be unrolled, not placed as a normal block</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Sleep Changes</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">You can lie in a bed without sleeping (right-click to lie, hold to sleep)</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">A HUD element shows the time remaining until you can sleep again</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Sleeping in the Nether causes a less intense explosion</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Two beds side by side act as one queen size bed</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Villagers close their eyes while sleeping nearby</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Fixes multiplayer players floating two pixels above beds</span></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Misc</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Moon banner pattern</strong>, for moon-charged banners in every color</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Sleep requirements, benefits, penalties and nightmare rates are configured separately for beds, hammocks and night bags</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Commands for reading and setting Home Bed nights, consecutive nights, sleep cooldown and nightmare chance</span></li>
</ul>
