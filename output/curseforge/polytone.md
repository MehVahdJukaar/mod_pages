<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>📖 About 📖</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Polytone is a client-side <strong>customization mod</strong> that allows extensive tweaking of nearly every visual and audio aspect of the game through <strong>Resource Packs</strong>.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">It also offers <strong>full backwards compatibility</strong> with Optifine's <code>color.properties</code> system, so existing packs work out of the box — while offering far more options than Optifine ever did.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Full documentation and an example pack are available on the <a href="https://github.com/MehVahdJukaar/polytone/wiki"><strong>Wiki</strong></a>. Feel free to add Polytone to the optional dependency section of your resource pack!</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px">Check the Files tab for actively supported versions.</span></p>

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/magenta.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<li><span style="font-size:18px">Custom <strong>Biome Colors & Particles</strong> 🌄</span></li>

<li><span style="font-size:18px">Custom <strong>Fluid Colormaps</strong> 🍸</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Custom <strong>Sky Colors</strong> 🌈</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Custom <strong>Map Colors</strong> 🗺️</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Change <strong>Spawn Egg Colors</strong> 🐣</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Day Time-Variant <strong>Animated Textures</strong> ☀️</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:18px">Block <strong>Offset Type</strong> 📐</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:24px"><strong>🔧 Compatibility 🔧</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Optifine:</strong> Polytone is a drop-in replacement for Optifine's <code>color.properties</code> system. Existing packs work automatically, and you gain many features Optifine never offered. See also: <a href="https://github.com/LambdAurora/optifine_alternatives">optifine_alternatives</a>.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Sodium:</strong> On 1.20, some features may not work with Sodium due to <a href="https://github.com/CaffeineMC/sodium-fabric/issues/2298">this upstream issue</a>. Consider using <strong>Embeddium</strong> instead for full compatibility.</span></p>
