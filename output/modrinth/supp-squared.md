<h2 style="text-align:center">📖 About 📖</h2>

Supplementaries² is the overflow drawer for [Supplementaries](https://modrinth.com/mod/supplementaries). It's where the extra color variants, the extra wood variants and the odd ideas that didn't quite earn a slot in the base mod ended up.

If you already build with Supplementaries and keep wishing a block came in one more flavour, this is that mod.

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/supp-squared.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<h2 style="text-align:center">📦 What's Inside 📦</h2>

- **Colored Sacks**: sacks in all 16 dye colors, dyeable in the crafting grid
- **Item Shelves in every wood type**, instead of just the vanilla few
- **Gold Candle Holders**: the candle holder in gold, in every candle color including soul, ender, spectacle and cupric
- **Daub Stairs, Slabs and Vertical Slabs**, for both plain daub and wattle and daub
- **Metal Frames**, plus braces and cross braces
- **Plaques** in iron, gold and copper: engravable signs with their own edit screen
- **Copper, Crimson, Gold and Brass Lanterns**
- **Heavy Key**: like a normal key, but it can't be renamed and is unique on craft, so it can't be duplicated
- **Weighted Doormats**

<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MehVahdJukaar/mod_pages/master/assets/separators/supp-squared.png" alt="separator" width="1688" height="42"></p>

<h2 style="text-align:center">❓ F.A.Q. ❓</h2>

**Q: Do I need Supplementaries?**

**A:** Yes. This is an addon, it does nothing on its own.

**Q: Why isn't this just part of Supplementaries?**

**A:** Supplementaries is already enormous. Splitting off the variants and the odder additions keeps the base mod's block count sane for people who don't want them.

**Q: Can I use this in my modpack?**

**A:** Go ahead.
