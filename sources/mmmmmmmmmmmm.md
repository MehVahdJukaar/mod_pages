## 📖 About 📖

MmmMmmMmmMmm adds exactly one item: a **Target Dummy**.

Place it in the world and it stands there silently, judging your every action. Hit it and it wiggles, screaming damage numbers at you. Dress it up in armor to please it - and make the numbers smaller.

[SEPARATOR]

## 🎯 Features 🎯

[YOUTUBE: sfYEYH5Haj4 | Showcase]

### 📦 Placement & Rotation

- Right-click on a block with a Target Dummy to place it
- 16 rotation directions based on where you're facing - like an armor stand
- Shift + left-click with an empty hand to pick it up

### 🛡️ Equipping & Armor

- Right-click to equip armor, tools, or shields - click the corresponding body part to unequip
- Equip an **undead mob head** to make it register as undead (for Smite testing)
- Other mob heads change its type for other damage categories
- Equip a **player head** to make mobs target the dummy instead of you
- Equip a **pumpkin or jack o'lantern** to activate **Scarecrow mode** - scares nearby animals

### 💥 Damage Display

- Damage numbers color-code by type: red for crits, yellow for lightning, etc.
- Supports **multiple simultaneous numbers** with a fading animation
- DPS shown on the status bar - choose between static or dynamic display in configs
- Rename the item to a number to set the dummy's health
- Enable damage numbers for **any entity** in the configs

### 👑 Boss Mode

- Give it a **banner** to enter Boss Mode - shows a boss health bar

### ⚙️ Automation

- Dispensers can place and remove dummies
- Remove by breaking the block under its feet
- Place a **Target Block** below the dummy to read damage dealt as a redstone signal
