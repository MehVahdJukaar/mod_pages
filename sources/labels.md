## 📖 About 📖

Labels mark what is inside your storage. They work like Item Frames, but sit flush against the block, stick to any surface, and let you interact with the block behind them.

Place one, then shift-click it with an item to set what it shows. Icons are drawn in a flat, hand-painted style rather than as a floating 3D item.

[SEPARATOR]

## 🏷️ Features 🏷️

- Stick to floors, ceilings, walls, slabs, stairs and fences, with no flat surface required
- Clicking a Label passes the interaction through to the block behind it, so labelled chests and barrels still open
- Apply a **Glow Ink Sac** to make one visible in the dark
- Dye them in any of the 16 colors
- Shift-click with a **Feather** to show the item's name instead of its icon
- Clicking with a stack updates the displayed item. Labels hold no inventory of their own
- Rendering style is configurable, and overridable with a resource pack

[SEPARATOR]

## ❓ F.A.Q. ❓

**Q: Can I put a Label on a chest without blocking access?**
**A:** Yes - clicking the Label interacts with the block behind it, so your chest opens normally.

**Q: Does it work on modded blocks?**
**A:** Labels can be placed on any surface that accepts a block entity interaction. Works with most modded storage blocks.
